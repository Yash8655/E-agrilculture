About the center

According to the government there are around 14 crore farmers in India and out of these only 3 crore knows how to use technology to their favour. Introducing an app or website and hoping that all the farmers get access to the data is pointless. So, to solve this problem the concept Agri Centre emerged. The Agri Centre will host all the farmers and middlemen in that area and will provide/collect data. The Mandi rates of all the states and districts will be displayed at the Agri Centre. These rates can help the farmers negotiate better.


About the Website

The website provides management capabilities to the employees of the Agri Centre to allow keeping record of the farmers in that area. Access to information also becomes easy as the data becomes centralized. Different forms regarding the yield, yield availability, tools/vehicles for rent and to rent also becomes available to all the parties. These forms can be filed at the centre itself. Different government schemes can be searched on the website and reference to the real context can be found. The data from the database can be used to match the required criteria and send SMS to alert or inform them about the government schemes
